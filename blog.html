Essential HVAC Maintenance Tips for Every Season

March 4, 2025

Keep your HVAC system running efficiently year-round with essential seasonal maintenance. Spring calls for cleaning filters, clearing debris, and checking condensate lines. Summer focuses on monitoring outdoor units and ensuring proper airflow. Fall preparation involves furnace inspection and duct maintenance, while winter requires protecting outdoor units and managing indoor humidity. Professional maintenance twice yearly is crucial for identifying potential issues and maintaining warranty requirements. Regular tasks include system inspection, electrical testing, and refrigerant checks. Follow these guidelines to extend your system's life, optimize performance, and maintain comfortable indoor temperatures throughout the year.
