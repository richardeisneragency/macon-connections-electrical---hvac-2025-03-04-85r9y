About Macon Connections

Founded in 2015, Macon Connections began with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality electrical and HVAC services to our community. What started as a small family business has grown into one of Macon's most trusted service providers, built on a foundation of technical expertise and unwavering customer service.

Our core values of integrity, excellence, and reliability guide everything we do. We believe in transparent pricing, honest recommendations, and building lasting relationships with our clients. Our team of certified professionals undergoes regular training to stay current with the latest industry technologies and safety standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We proudly serve the greater Macon area and surrounding communities within a 50-mile radius, including Warner Robins, Perry, and Byron.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services for both electrical and HVAC issues. Our average response time is under 60 minutes for urgent situations.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. All our technicians are certified and undergo regular training to maintain the highest service standards.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and checks. We also offer flexible financing options for larger projects with approved credit.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans for both HVAC and electrical systems. These plans include regular inspections, priority service, and exclusive discounts.
